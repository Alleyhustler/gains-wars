GALACTIC RULES

  • During Entry Phase (15 min), submit proof of your token holdings with Solscan TX
  • After entry closes, Battle Phase begins (30 min max duration)
  • All participants vote for the wallet with the smallest holdings
  • The holder with most votes gets eliminated immediately
  • Process repeats until only one holder remains
  • Last remaining holder wins the prize pool (random 5-10 SOL)
  • New rounds start automatically every 2 hours
  • No wallet connection needed - just prove your holdings

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Buy the memecoin (contract: TBA)
  2. Wait for entry phase to open
  3. Submit your wallet address and TX proof
  4. Defend your Gains during battle phase
  5. Eliminate weaker holders to survive
